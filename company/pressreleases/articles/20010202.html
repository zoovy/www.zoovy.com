Business Web Newsletter - February 2, 2001

 by Jinger Jarrett

Web Site: Zoovy.com - http://www.zoovy.com. Although this web site has a rather unusual name, I would have to say that I would recommend this site to anyone who is looking to engage in ecommerce, especially someone who is new to the world of online marketing. Why?

1. This site has a very nice, clean interface and is easy to navigate. By navigate, I mean it didn't take me long at all to find out what the site was about, and how it could help me. No fluff, no hype, just solutions.

So what is this site for? It is an ecommerce solution, especially if you are looking to put your business on the World Wide Web. For those who don't have a site, Zoovy will build you one from the ground up and customize it to your specifications. This includes things like shopping carts, a catalog, domain name hosting, and more. Once your are set up, Zoovy says it is committed to making your business a success with promotion tools and more.

Packages start at $20, making it affordable for even us small business people. The nice thing about this solution is that you also get a free trial, greatly reducing your risk if you aren't happy with the solution.

Definitely worth taking a look at if you are in the market for a new place to put your web site or build a new solution for your business.
Highly Recommended.
