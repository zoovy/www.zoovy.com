Zoovy, Inc. is First Auction Management and e-Commerce Company to be Approved as UPS OnLine® Tools Product Provider
ups

Zoovy, Inc., a leading provider of online auction management and e-Commerce solutions, today announced it has become an approved UPS OnLine® Tools Product Provider.

Zoovy's integration with UPS OnLine Tools, the first for any auction technology provider, allows Zoovy merchants to provide their customers with real-time shipping quotes, tracking information and address validation, eliminating many of the complications potentially involved with doing business online.

"Our partnership with UPS is another example of our commitment to consistently provide technology-based solutions that solve real problems for our clients and their customers", said David Steel, CEO of Zoovy, Inc. "UPS OnLine Tools solve many of the issues that inhibit the growth on online sales and represents a tremendous cost and time savings potential for online merchants and auction sellers."

Customers using the integrated solution can easily access critical shipping information without leaving the Zoovy storefront. UPS OnLine Tools add a new level of customer service and functionality to a company's enterprise and web applications on a global scale.
