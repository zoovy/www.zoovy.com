Zoovy, Inc. and InterShopZone Announce Strategic Agreement
Zoovy merchants gain increased exposure through InterShopZone Marketplace
San Diego, CA & Haverstraw, NY
intershopzone

Zoovy, Inc., an auction management and e-commerce company that empowers businesses to reach dynamic online markets and increase sales, now supports InterShopZone, an all-in-one business and consumer services network that offer business and merchant services, as well as a marketplace for buyers and sellers alike. The agreement enables merchants using Zoovy to effortlessly sell products at InterShopZone, drawing exposure for their products and generating additional sales.

"This agreement provides our merchants with an incredible opportunity to maximize their online presence," said David Steel, CEO of Zoovy. "InterShopZone has a large user base looking for bargains on brand name merchandise. We believe InterShopZone is an excellent venue for our merchants to generate additional sales."

"Frequent sellers of InterShopZone have been looking for auction management software that incorporates many of the unique and exciting features that the Zoovy system currently offers. We believe that the Zoovy service will only enhance our users’ experience even further," said Michael Finestein, director of business development for InterShopZone.

The marketplace at InterShopZone strives to offer the best products and services that are currently available, and the addition of Zoovy merchants will enable our marketplace to do just that. This new service addition to InterShopZone helps the company offer the management services that many sellers on competing marketplaces need in order to run their businesses efficiently. InterShopZone sees this addition to our network as just another service that shows our commitment to our marketplace, our sellers, and of course our industry.
