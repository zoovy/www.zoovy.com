Zoovy Continues Growth; 10,000 Customers Strong

Zoovy Inc., today announced that it has grown to over 10,000 users. Last month, Zoovy launched its lead product, Zoovy Merchant.

"With the help of Cardiff-based Zoovy Inc., small businesses can find themselves successfully going cyber toe to toe with the big guys when building an operating an online store," raved Peggy Scott in a recent San Diego Union-Tribune article.

Zoovy is unique in its field because it offers both web-access and Windows software to access the administrative functions of their online storefronts. Some distinctive new features include advanced label and invoice printing, the ability to combine and split orders, and customized HTML email messages.

"Sales and productivity have gone way up," said Kathy Kilna, owner of Kathy Kilna's Creations. "I have almost tripled my sales since I've used Zoovy. It saves me so much time."

About Zoovy Inc.
