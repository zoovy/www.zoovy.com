Zoovy, Inc. Announces Their Online Business Management Solutions Now Share Data with Intuit’s QuickBooks 2003® Products
intuit

Zoovy, Inc., the leading provider of right-sized e-commerce™ solutions, today announced it now offers data integration between Zoovy’s proprietary e-commerce web storefront, on-line payment processing, auction management, inventory management, sales order processing, and customer relationship management tools, and Intuit’s QuickBooks 2003 products – including QuickBooks Pro 2003, QuickBooks Premier 2003 and QuickBooks Enterprise Solutions.

Zoovy’s data integration with QuickBooks 2003 products allows Zoovy’s e-commerce solutions to interoperate automatically with QuickBooks, which can result in faster and more efficient data processing. Zoovy’s solutions are geared to businesses of all sizes, looking to quickly and efficiently participate in dynamic marketplaces such as eBay, Dealtime, Yahoo and Amazon. Zoovy offers packaged and custom solutions, including its patented technology, training and support services; to fit businesses of all sizes from SME’s to enterprise applications.

"QuickBooks is by far the most widely used accounting package for our sellers," said David Steel, CEO of Zoovy, Inc. "We are extremely pleased to be working directly with Intuit to provide a seamless way for our merchants to integrate their accounting activity with their QuickBooks applications." Zoovy provides businesses with a total auction management software solution that allows merchants to launch, track, manage and process auctions all through their Zoovy eCommerce system.
