Marketing Internship

Job Description:

Zoovy, Inc, the leading provider of online auction management and e-Commerce solutions, is seeking an Marketing Intern to help with efforts to further brand awareness and new customer acquisition.

Job Responsibilities:

- Review current copy on web and paper - suggest and make changes
- Write Press Releases and follow-up
- Conduct market research
- Conduct customer satisfaction surveys
- Development of marketing materials

Preferred candidate qualifications include:

- A background in marketing
- Experience in market research (or academic coursework in market research)
- Experience writing press releases
- Excellent analytical and communication skills
- Familiarity or interest in e-commerce helpful

Internship offers flexible hours and could last between 1-3 months, with potential for a long term opportunity.
