Implementation Specialist


This is a key role in the pre-sales, and implementation phases of Zoovy's e-commerce platform into customers business. Requires high degree of technical and software knowledge and user interaction. Must have ability to interact with customers and translate customer requests to software specifications and work independently to solve technical problems.

Responsibilities include analyzing client needs on-the-fly, creating and proposing technology implementations to the clients and then managing implementation of those solutions to meet our clients' diverse needs. The position is an interesting mix of technical sales and client-facing work and the successful candidate will be enthusiastic about both areas.

This position does not require cold calling. This is an inside sales position.

As an Implementation Engineer you Will:

  • Work directly with the client's business and technical personnel to map requirements to a technical strategy.
  • Work closely with inside sales staff to help them achieve sales targets and directly improve client retention through better business needs assessment.
  • Provide limited ongoing support to implemented clients.
  • Work closely with multiple groups within Zoovy to drive product direction and implementation services.
  • Report directly to the CEO and/or Sales Manager.

Some Responsibilities Include:

  • Serve as the technical point of contact between the customer and all service, sales and project team members during the installation process.
  • Resolve application and networking technical problems, analyze and evaluate issues that may occur prior to and during implementation when interfacing with customers existing systems.
  • Present technical training to customers in both pre-sales and limited post-sales role.
  • Supports sales/marketing and includes on-site troubleshooting, sales demonstrations, customer training and commissioning.

Requirements:

  • Technical background including e-commerce consulting or development experience.
  • Ability to analyze website construction and prepare quotes to build equivalent, or enhanced functionality using Zoovy's Content Management system.
  • Experience with data formats such as CSV, common Image formats, HTML.
  • Familiarity with eBay, and other related e-commerce sites.
  • Excellent customer service, presentation and writing skills. Must be comfortable interacting with both small and medium sized clients. Project Management experience is a plus.
  • Outstanding problem solving skills and attention to detail. Must be able to think creatively about issues to find a resolution.
  • The ability to learn quickly. You will have to become an expert on the product in a short amount of time.
  • Limited programming experience in any language (especially at a conceptual level) would also be useful.
  • Ability to understand e-commerce fulfillment process, warehouse management, supply chain/logistics, SEO, and other e-commerce related disciplines.

Salary Structure:

$36,000 - $48,000/yr base (based on experience) + Bonus (up to $28k/yr - paid quarterly)

Your bonus is based on your ability to assist our internal sales staff in achieving their sales targets. You win when you help them win. Subsequently having some sales experience will make you more successful in this position.

Sales team has established quarterly goals based on the ability to achieve new account sales targets, client retention, and existing client sales.

To Apply:

Email questions or resume to jobs@zoovy.com
