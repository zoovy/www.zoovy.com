Product Support Specialist II


Job Description:

Provides technical support to customers and sales staff with regard to the company's products and service. Also performs maintenance, bug verification, release testing, and beta support for assigned products. Requires knowledge of basic function of the company's products as well as other customer applications. Operates under general supervision. Reports to Chief Technology Officer. Typically requires 1 to 3 years of technical support and/or website design experience.

Essential Duties and Responsibilities

 - Assists customers in design and usage of Zoovy e-Commerce platform.
 - Provides assistance to website developers in release testing, bug verification, and beta support.
 - Performs in-house testing of applications, in real-world scenarios.

Essential Education, Skills and Environment

 - Education and Work Experience
 - Typically requires one to three years of technical support and/or product design experience.

Specialized Knowledge and Skills

 - Good knowledge of products with ability to work under general supervision on moderately complex technical tasks, as well as on-site product installations and consulting projects.
 - May be able to operate on several computer platforms and environments.
 - Good communication, interpersonal, organizational and planning skills.
 - Basic knowledge of HTML and Internet protocols.
 - Good PC skills.

Equipment and Applications

 - PC and related software applications.
 - Heavy (6+ hours per day) telephone usage.

To Apply:

Email questions or resume to jobs@zoovy.com
