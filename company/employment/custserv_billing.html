Customer Service/Billing Administrator

Zoovy Inc., an e-commerce software company in Carlsbad, is seeking an Administrative pro to join our team. The primary function will be as the point person for a high touch customer contact environment, while offering assistance in billing and customer retention. In addition, you will be responsible for administrative sales support and other various administrative duties as assigned for all Zoovy departments. Excellent customer service and strong verbal communication skills a must. Previous administrative and MS Office experience required. Prior billing experience a plus. Send resumes with salary requirements to: Jobs@zoovy.com

Duties and Responsibilities:

  • Directly call a minimum of 50 customers per day to follow up on their Zoovy experience
  • Provide customer retention techniques to attempt to save customers that are requesting to cancel
  • Be the intermediary in billing inquiries, questions, and disputes between the billing deptartment and Zoovy customers
  • Track all daily credits and cancellations and provide a daily report to the Sales Manager, Billing Manager and CEO
  • Provide a bi-weekly account activity report to Sales Manager, Billing Manager and CEO
  • Manage all inbound calls and direct customers to the appropriate department or individual
  • Coordinate all necessary arrangements for company travel, tradeshows, or internal company functions
  • Other duties as assigned

Required Experience/Skills

  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Previous billing experience
  • QuickBooks accounting software experience
  • Type at least 50 WPM
  • 10 key experience
  • Ability to provide a professional verbal presentation
  • Excellent organizational skills
  • Ability to multi-task in a fast paced environment
  • Adaptable to a highly dynamic environment

Zoovy offers excellent benefits and a casual, family-oriented work environment. If you are interested in joining a dynamic and quickly growing company, please send your resume AND salary requirements/history.

To Apply:

Email questions or resume to jobs@zoovy.com
