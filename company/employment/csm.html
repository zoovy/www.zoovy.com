Customer Service Manager

Job Description:

This position is responsible for coordinating and supervising the Customer Service department including dealing with personnel issues, scheduling and work assignments, assist in dealing with customers, supervises the implementation of both long and short-term projects, and conducts employee performance reviews.

Job Requirements:

  • A minimum of 2-3 years' prior experience as a customer service supervisor.
  • Excellent interpersonal skills
  • Customer relation skills
  • Organizational skills
  • Strong and proven analytical skills
  • IT technical skills

Essential Duties and Responsibilities

  • Assign new incoming "Fast Track" accounts to primary account managers
  • Track the fulfillment of Training materials to customer.
  • Track account progress, provide feedback and advice to account managers.
  • Accept work requests from account managers and assign jobs to product support specialists.
  • Oversee the completion of technical support calls and emails within satisfactory times to meet service goals.
  • Drive growth by assisting sales with project planning and resource estimation.
  • Coordinate with development on status of projects for timeline purposes.
  • Other duties as needed.

Pay is commensurate based on experience, if you feel you are the correct person for this job please email your resume, along with a brief cover letter and salary history to the address below.
