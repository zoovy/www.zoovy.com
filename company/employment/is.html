Inside Sales Representative (Telesales)

Job Description:

Zoovy, Inc, the leading provider of online auction management and e-Commerce solutions, is seeking to expand its Inside Sales Staff to take advantage of the response to our latest product offerings.

Job Responsibilities:

100% Inside Sales - The Telesales position is responsible for the sales of Zoovy e-Commerce services. The position requires professional, experienced Inside Sales Representatives who will interface with business owners via phone, fax and e-mail. This position is commission based and includes a monthly guarantee. The Telesales position reports to the Director of Sales.

We have an immediate need for experienced Inside Sales Representatives who will be responsible for responding to inbound telephone/email inquiries and initiating calls to potential clients; promoting and selling our products and services. A 4-year degree is preferred, but not mandatory for this position. The Inside Sales Rep. must be able to work well independently.

Job Requirements:

 -  Minimum 1-3 year.s sales/account management experience
 -  Experience in a high-paced sales environment (internet exp. preferred)
 -  Working knowledge of Internet, database management, and Microsoft Office programs
 -  Excellent communication skills both verbal and written.
 -  Ability to work well individually, as well as part of a team
 -  Comfortable working in a small entrepreneurial, fast paced environment
 -  Strong verbal and written communication skills and the ability to interact with others and exhibit a positive attitude.

To Apply:

Email questions or resume to jobs@zoovy.com
