Account Executive

The successful Inside Sales Manager is a high-energy, focused on building consultative relationships at all levels within a B2C retail e-commerce platform solution. They are motivated by solving business problems on behalf of prospective customer and ultimately driving the adoption of Zoovy’s e-Commerce solutions throughout the e-commerce enterprise. The Inside Sales Manager enjoys uncovering and closing new business opportunities in a start-up environment. They must be a strategic with exceptional closing skills. Willingness to learn a structured solution selling process. They are goal-oriented with a desire to win.

Responsibilities:

  • Identify, uncover, and develop account opportunities within a given territory.
  • Excellent ability to properly qualify prospects to maximize an efficient sales cycle. Accurately forecast and close e-retail brands to exceed customer acquisition goals.
  • Modify and deliver sales presentations and demonstrations.
  • Leverage relationships with Zoovy Partners within your territory.

Requirements:

  • Must have 2+ years of experience selling e-commerce platforms or retail solutions.
  • Successful background in complex,executive-level sales processes
  • Must have a consistent record of quota achievement Must have experience in a team sales environment and excel at team building.
  • Demonstrate excellent verbal and written communication skills. A technical background and aptitude is important. Strong time management, organization, and decision making skills are critical.

About Zoovy:

Zoovy is a 10 year old profitable company based in Carlsbad California focused on full turnkey e-Commerce solutions. Our offering includes a complete multi-channel ready storefronts; secure shopping cart, auction integration, marketing assistance, design and graphics, in-house consulting and content management tools developed specifically for ecommerce business owners. A fun group with social activities such as a co-ed softball team.
