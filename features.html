FEATURES - We have 'em all

Our e-commerce solution has all of the features and add-ons that you can imagine... and some you can't! With the flexibility of the app-framework, you have the freedom to do business your way. Explore some of the 800+ features we provide:

Top Advanced features:

Amazon

  • Seamless synchronization with webstore listings with Amazon including product options such as size, color etc.
  • Product mapping - Eliminating the need to map each product individually
  • Bi-Directional Integration for UPS, FED-X and USPS tracking, automatically confirms and order has shipped

eBay

  • Increased exposure and sales
  • Easy inventory management
  • Seamless integration with all major shipping and payment providers

Variance (aka A/B Testing)

  • Included in the Zoovy package.
  • A/B testing is a must power feature for the advance ecommerce sites in today’s market today
  • Give a clear understanding of your specific customers needs and buying traits
  • Integrated into Zoovy, not a bolt on.
  • Google Analytics friendly

Mobile Commerce

  • No phone limitations other than HTML and Java Script enabled
  • Mature feature as Zoovy merchants have been using for over two years with their customers
  • Largest network
  • Built in reports to enable continuous optimization

Order Management

  • Real-time integrations into payment services including Authorize.net and PayPal
  • Automatically generated email confirmations
