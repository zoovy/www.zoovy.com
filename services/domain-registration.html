Domain Registration

Merchants have the option to use Zoovy as a domain registar.


Benefits:

  • Automatic renewals, which means no down time due to expiration
  • Added security, DNS can not be changed without Zoovy being aware
  • Purchase domains directly through Zoovy interface

Zoovy becomes the registar, however merchant remains registered owner.
