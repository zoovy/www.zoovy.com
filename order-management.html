Order Management

Manage Your business operations including sales channel management,
transaction and order management, CRM and reporting.

  • Integrates all stages of an order into one system
  • Order processing is automated, fully integrated and customizable
  • Instant creation and notification of new orders from all sales channels
  • Flexible rules based order staging
  • Multiple payment methods supported
  • Real-time integrations into payment services including Authorize.net and PayPal
  • Automatically sent email confirmations
  • Modification of order even after order is placed
