With Zoovy, you can easily launch your product into eBay, eBay Motors or your eBay Store. Launch items one at a time or use our power listing tool to automate launching/relaunching and let the inventory system stop launching when needed.

Use eBay to increase your sales in your store.

  • Checkout occurs through your store, so you can upsell auction winners
  • Market to past auction winners using our CRM tools
  • Allows a single checkout for winners of multiple items

Inventory management of your powerlisters

Once you set up a powerlister auction, the inventory system can handle it from that point on. Set a few parameters for how many auctions you want listed at the same time and the auction will relist automatically until inventory is low.
