Helpful Tips for the Online Product Editor

The new Ajax powered Zoovy Product editor has been designed for safer and more intuitive data-entry allowing you to effectively keep track of the product information you need to publish live to your Zoovy store.

Much faster page rendering - Quicker, Slicker.

Sellers with very large product databases will notice significant speed increases in page rendering saving you time when adding product to your large database.

Lost data during entry - A thing of the past.

The potential for data loss during entry is virtually eliminated as each section allows you to 'Save-as-you-go' by either pressing the save button or simply collapsing the section, leaving you to focus on the remaining information that you wish to edit.

Collapsible Information Sections - Focus only on what you need.

With so many features and options available for each of your products, making sure you have entered all the information needed can be hard to keep track of. Now with new collapsible information areas, you can simply focus on the information you need to insert and collapse the other options from view. The system will remember which windows you collapse and which stay open, allowing you to customize the form for future data entry only showing the information that is relevant to you.

Cleaner, easy-to-read layout - Everything you need, nothing you don't.

The new layout allows you to view your product information without being distracted by other objects on the page.

Why the change? Because the future of the web is knocking at the door.

Ajax web technology is going to change the way the entire internet will look and work, and it's coming into play at breakneck speeds (follow the exploits of Google and Microsoft over the next few months and you'll see what we mean). To take advantage of the coming revolution make sure you have the latest version of your preferred internet browser installed for maximum compatibility.
