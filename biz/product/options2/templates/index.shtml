Product specific option groups (also known as POGs) can only be used by a single product (not shared across products).

To share one or more options across multiple products first create a Store Option Group (or SOG for short). Using POGs is not recommended unless the set of options you are specifying are unique to this product exclusively. POG's cannot be used with certain types of syndication such as Amazon.

Max: 26 POGs per product

Store option groups (or SOGs for short) is a set of shared options which can be applied to multiple products.

A product can be setup to automatically inherit options in a SOG (e.g. the options are the same for every product), or selectively choose which of the options are available on a product by product basis (e.g. there are 6 possible sizes, but a particular product might use 3).

Max: 936 SOGs per store. To create a store option group click on the "Store Option Groups" link on the left hand navigation bar of the online product editor.
