Inventory Control provides the following features:
* Track the quantity of item's you have in stock.
* Automatically have products removed from your website when the quantity reaches zero, or a minimum safety quantity.
* Improve customer service by disclosing inventory status (eg: in stock) or inventory quantity levels.
* Keep a good handle on your supply chain by tracking multiple SKU's into a single product.
