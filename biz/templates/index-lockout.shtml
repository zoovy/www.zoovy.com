Restriction of Access due to Repeated Billing Failure

 

Your account has been disabled due to repeated billing failures. Please review the information below for further instructions.

Possible reasons for disabling your account:
  1. Repeated Invalid or Incorrect Billing Information
  2. Significant Overdue Balance
  3. Zoovy has received a charge back on your account.

To have your account reinstated you must bring your account up to date and provide accurate billing information.

Your store operation has not been affected at this time, customers can still browse your web site and place orders, and you will still receive email confirmations from them. All of your channels should also be running properly at this time. However, you will not be able to access orders until this administrative lock is removed. However if you do not take care of this matter immediately then your account will be cancelled and removed from the system.


You should contact the Billing Department as soon as possible by calling 877-966-8948 extension 4, or via email at Billing@Zoovy.com
