Partition # Configuration
Call Center Module:
type="checkbox" name="private_callcenter"> Enable Private Callcenter
Private call centers require a unique login/luser per operator and are primarily intended for companies operating their own call centers with less than 10 agents total. Each agent must be named and may only be logged in at one terminal consecutively.
type="checkbox" name="public_callcenter"> Enable Public Callcenter(s)
Public call centers typically have more than 10 agents (possibly thousands), and allow agents to be authenticated by IP address and a simple operator id. Call center vendors should contact Zoovy business development to make licensing arrangements.
