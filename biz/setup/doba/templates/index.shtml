Adding Products via DOBA.ZOOVY.COM
Zoovy has created a Zoovy Store (http://doba.zoovy.com) which contains a full copy of the DOBA catalog. We have created you a username and password in the DOBA.ZOOVY.COM store.

DOBA.ZOOVY.COM USERNAME: admin@.zoovy.com
DOBA.ZOOVY.COM PASSWORD:

For security reasons your login and password are different on doba.zoovy.com (where you will add products) and doba.com (where you will approve orders).

You will add products to your store from doba.zoovy.com using the JEDI (Just Easy Data Integration) functionality. JEDI allows *ANY* two Zoovy stores to share products and will automatically synchronize the shipping, inventory, and ordering information.

You can also login directly to your account at http://DOBA.ZOOVY.COM just click here


Processing Orders through DOBA.COM
When orders that contain one or more doba items are flagged as paid your Zoovy store will automatically transmit the order to doba.zoovy.com using JEDI.

The transmission status will appear in the Utilities | Supply Chain manager in your Zoovy store. The orders will also appear instantly in http://doba.zoovy.com, and within 2-3 minutes they will be transmitted from doba.zoovy.com, to doba.com.

Once the order has arrived at doba.com you will need to login to your doba.com account and approve the order. Unfortunately Doba does not allow Zoovy to automatically approve orders on your behalf.
