Insurance


Insurance fees for orders will be:
value="0"> Not Used/Disabled.
value="1"> Included as part of the shipping total.
value="2"> Displays as it's own line item under shipping.
*NOTE: If you combine insurance as part of shipping - it cannot be reported on separately and may affect some aspects of reporting.
Insurance is:
value="1"> Optional (user will be prompted)
value="0"> Mandatory (will be automatically included in order)

Insurance fees are applied after the rules based shipping calculation. Insurance fees are typically used to cover the cost of 3rd party insurance companies, or as a profit center for companies who decide to be "self insuring". You may enable as many insurance calculations as you like, all methods will be summed together to arrive at the final insurance total.

name=""> Per Product:
     Enabling a per product handling will allow you to specify a different handling charge for each product (stored as zoovy:ship_insurance). Each handling charge will only be applied once per product, regardless of how many are purchased.

value="0"> Disabled (do not configure handling per product)
value="7"> Use per product for all destinations
value="1"> Use per product for domestic orders only
value="2"> Use per product for canada orders only
value="4"> Use per product for international orders only
value="6"> Use per product for canada + international orders
