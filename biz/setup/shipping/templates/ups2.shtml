UPS Configuration

Attention Existing Customers: Please review the settings below, once you press save they will take effect immediately. The interface has been updated to come into compliance with new UPS branding and trademark requirements. One major change is that UPS quotes will NOT calculate if any other shipping method has been selected.

name="ups_dom"> Enable United Parcel Service Domestic (Inside the United States) Shipping Support
name="ups_int"> Enable United Parcel Service International Shipping Support

USAGE:   Please select the method(s) of United Parcel Service shipping you want to offer to your customers. During checkout the price will be derived from UPS based on the total weight of the order, and making the assumption that it will fit into a standard box size.

Your company address MUST be configured in your account setup for this method to accurately calculate shipping from your origin to the customers destination address.

The handling fee (if any) will be added to the UPS quoted rates, and will cause a message to be displayed that the rates quoted do not reflect actual UPS shipping rates.
