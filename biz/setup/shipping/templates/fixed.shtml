Simple Multiple Item Shipping: this shipping method accepts two shipping prices per product. A first item shipping price, and a second item shipping price. On multi-item orders the first price is used for the item with the most expensive shipping price, and the second item price for all other items.

Shipping Description:
name="simplemulti_dom"> Enable for Domestic (All 50 states) [Shipping Rules]
Canada Shipping Description:
name="simplemulti_can"> Enable for Canada [Shipping Rules]
