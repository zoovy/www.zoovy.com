Handling


Handling fees for orders will be:
value="0"> Not Used/Disabled.
value="1"> Included as part of the shipping total.
value="2"> Displays as it's own line item under shipping.
*NOTE: If you combine handling as part of shipping - it cannot be reported on separately and may affect some aspects of reporting.

Handling fees are applied to all shipping methods after the rules based shipping calculation. Handling fees are typically used to cover the packaging expenses and/or labor involved in assembling an order. You may enable as many handling calculations as you like, all methods will be summed together to arrive at the final handling total.

name="flat">Flat Handling Per Item: NOTE: This method works off the total count of all items in the cart, if a customer purchases qty 2 of one item, and qty 3 of another, this would charge the first item price, plus the second item price for each of the remaining qty of 4.

name=""> Per Product:
     Enabling a per product handling will allow you to specify a different handling charge for each product (stored as zoovy:ship_handling). Each handling charge will only be applied once per product, regardless of how many are purchased.

value="0"> Disabled (do not configure handling per product)
value="7"> Use per product for all destinations
value="1"> Use per product for domestic orders only
value="2"> Use per product for canada orders only
value="4"> Use per product for international orders only
value="6"> Use per product for canada + international orders


name="weight"> Weight Based:
     A Weight Based calculation will sum the total weight of all the items in the cart combined and then use a lookup table to determine the next highest handling fee.
