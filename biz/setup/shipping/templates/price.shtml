Simple Price Calculator allows you to specify a handling rate based on the subtotal of an order. During checkout the least value, which is greater than the total cart price (before shipping, tax, etc.) will match. If the cart costs more than the greatest amount, and no other shipping methods are available then the message "Actual Shipping To Be Determined" will be displayed.
