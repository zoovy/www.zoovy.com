USAGE:   Please select the method(s) of Federal Express shipping you want to offer to your customers. During checkout the price will be derived from Federal Express based on your account number and the total weight of the order, making the assumption that the contents will fit into a standard box size. Your company address MUST be configured in your account setup for this method to accurately calculate shipping from your origin to the customers destination address. The handling fee (if any) will be added to the Federal Express quoted rates.
