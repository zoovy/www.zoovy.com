Rating Configuration:
Please select the method(s) of FedEx shipping you want to offer to your customers. During checkout the price will be derived from FedEx based on your account number and the total weight of the order, making the assumption that all the contents will fit into a standard box size.

FedEx International Settings
name="int" value="on"> Enable FedEx® International Shipping Support [general rules]

Quote Domestic Packaging Type As:

Ground will always be quoted as "Other Packaging"

The more of these shipping methods you allow, the slower the checkout page will be.

name="int_nextearly" value="1"> FedEx International First® [specific rules]
name="int_nextnoon" value="1"> FedEx International Priority® [specific rules]
name="int_2day" value="1"> FedEx International Economy®* [specific rules]
name="int_ground" value="1"> FedEx International Ground® [specific rules]
* This shipping method does not support certain packaging, and will assume the FedEx® Pak price if Envelopes, 10kg or 25kg boxes are selected.

Other Considerations:
  • Specific rules only take effect on the shipping type they are created for, whereas general rules will be applied to each available shipping method.
  • Currently no rating support is provided for quoting Saturday delivery, or Freight.
