MANDATORY: In order to use this feature you MUST have a Federal Express Account number. If you do not put in an account number then Zoovy will default to a location of Cardiff by the Sea, CA.

Origin Location Fedex Account Number:

USAGE:   Please select the method(s) of Federal Express shipping you want to offer to your customers. During checkout the price will be derived from Federal Express based on your account number and the total weight of the order, making the assumption that the contents will fit into a standard box size. Your company address MUST be configured in your account setup for this method to accurately calculate shipping from your origin to the customers destination address. The handling fee (if any) will be added to the Federal Express quoted rates.

name="fedex_dom" value="on"> Enable Fedex Domestic Shipping Support

name="fedex_dom_3day" value="1"> Three Day Express Edit/View Advanced Rules

Simple Additional Handling Charge:
Note: having a simple handling charge will DISABLE all rule processing, if you have no rules and want faster shipping calculation then set this field to 0 (zero).
