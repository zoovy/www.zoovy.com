What is this?
You have been brought to this page in order generate a new eBay Authorization Token. eBay Authorization Tokens permit Zoovy to communicate with your eBay account. eBay Authorization Tokens increase security on your eBay account because no storage of your password is required.

Creating a new eBay Authorization Token is easy, simply on click the URL below, sign into your eBay account, and follow the steps.
