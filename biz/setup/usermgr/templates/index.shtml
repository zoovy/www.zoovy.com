Best Practices:
Only one user may be logged in at a time per account. Zoovy recommends one account per employee for the best security. Employees which have access to sensitive data should never have shared accounts or it will be impossible for you to accurately determine the root cause of a mistake or data mis-appropriation.

Please refer to your package details to learn how many users are included with your account or contact billing@zoovy.com. If you exceed the number included with your account you will be charged additional fees.

Users here also provide seat licenses for the desktop clients (although you may need to upgrade to network database licenses to have multiple employees use them concurrently).
