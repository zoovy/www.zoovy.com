What is possible?
As with most developer features the only limitation you have is what your mind can dream up, and your programmers can accomplish, but here is a common list of solutions afforded by the External Shipping API:

  • Real-time Integration with a custom order processing backend.
  • Real-time Integration with a fulfillment or shipping center.
  • Real-time Integration with a custom reporting system.
  • Integration with a variety of other 3rd party order processing applications such as e-Bridge (www.ebridgesoft.com)
    How does this work?
    Order Dispatching lets you easily integrate your Zoovy store with a variety of other
    great services such as automatically routing invoices to suppliers, sending orders to
    fulfillment centers and much more!

    If you think you might have a need for Order Dispatch services, or you have an application
    you think might benefit from Order Dispatch services please contact Zoovy Sales and let us know!
