Upload JEDI Package
JEDI allows a supplier to create a package which can be easily imported, once, or continuously into a zoovy store.

Initial Import Only:
name=".create"> Create a supplier in my database.
name=".themes"> Import themes, and layouts from package (if available)
JEDI Customer Account:
JEDI Customer Password
Customer Account / Password are the same as the ones you use to login to the store.

JEDI Products:
value="reset"> Full Reset: deletes all products from supplier, replaces with updated descriptions/images.
value="smart"> Prices Only: reloads prices, but does not make any changes.
value="new"> New Products: only loads new products that do not exist.

JEDI Inventory:
name=".inventory"> Refresh inventory from supplier.

Website Navigation Categories:
value="reset"> Full Reset: delete all categories and load new categories + pages from jedi package.
value="smart"> Partial: loads products into your site, if the category exists on your site.
value="ignore"> Leave my categories alone, I will map products manually.

Ordering:
value="yes"> Automatically place orders with supplier for paid items.
value="no"> I will manually place orders at vendors site.
value=""> Leave my settings alone.

Shipping:
value="yes"> Automatically ask my supplier for shipping rates whenever possible.
value="no"> I will configure my site shipping to calculate my shipping charges.
value=""> Leave my settings alone.
