Click here to add Domain Hosting to your Account.

Domain Hosting lets you host one or more custom domain names which can point to your Zoovy store. Zoovy will also forward one or more mail accounts so you and your employees can send and receive mail from employee@yourname.com.

If you want to have a domain name like http://www.my-company.com then you need to contact Zoovy technical support and inquire about purchasing domain redirection services.

If you do not already own your domain name, you will still need to reserve it (in addtion to buying hosting). Zoovy offers registration services for $10/yr.
