Forward Responses To:
How the "Forward Responses To" email address works: After a newsletter is sent - all unsubscribe requests such as bounces, and "remove" subject lines will be automatically handled by Zoovy and will NOT be forwarded.

All other emails received in response to a newsletter will be sent to the "Forward Responses" email address. If you leave the "Forward Responses" email address blank, then those emails will be discarded.

(Hint: if you configure an AutoResponder message below then that autoresponse will still be sent to the customer).

Autoresponse Text:

Leave the AutoResponder Text blank if you do not want to send an auto-response when a person replies to your newsletter. Note: The AutoResponse message is NOT sent to unsubscribe or bounce requests.
