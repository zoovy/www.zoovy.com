Useful Tips:
  • Credit cards are the preferred method of payment online.
  • Accepting only Offline methods such as check, wire transfers, etc. will reduce the number of potential buyers for your products.
  • Many online shoppers prefer to use wallets such as Paypal, Google Checkout and Amazon Payments.
  • To process credit cards online you will need both a merchant account, and a gateway. A merchant account is provided by a bank, or MSP (Merchant Service Provider), and a gateway connects your Merchant Account to the Internet.
  • Paypal provides three different types of service: Express Checkout (Paypal Wallet + Credit Card processing), Paypal Website Payments (Credit Card Processing + Gateway), and Verisign/Paypal Payflow Pro (Gateway Only).
