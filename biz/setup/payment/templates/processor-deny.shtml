ABOUT THIS AREA:
    This is this area where you would setup your Zoovy account to automatically process credit cards. A merchant account lets you easily accept credit cards.

There are two ways to use a merchant account with Zoovy: Manual and Payment Gateway.
1. With Manual processing Zoovy will take credit cards during checkout, and simply validate the card number and expiration date and then save the cardholder information with the order - it will be your responsibility to authorize payment using your own software or terminal and then flag the order as paid.
To enable this simply select the credit cards you will accept in the credit tab of the payment section.

2. With a Payment Gateway, Zoovy will automatically authorize cards at the time the order is placed so you don"t have to do anything. Zoovy currently supports several payment gateways, call technical support for details.
