Credit Card Acceptance Policy:
CVV/CID:
Please note: capturing and storing of the CVV/CID # is strictly prohibited by most merchant agreements. The CVV/CID should be supplied by the cardholder at the time of the transaction to increase security by ensuring the physical card is present. If CVV/CID is stored, then it can be copied - and subsequently stolen (which defeats the purpose of the entire CVV/CID security system). Consequently capturing this information could result in you being liable for any fraud that occurs as a result of your negligence. You should contact your merchant account provider BEFORE enabling this setting.
