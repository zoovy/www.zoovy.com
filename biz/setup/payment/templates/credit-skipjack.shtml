Important Usage Notes:
  • Before using this service you MUST contact SkipJack support (support@skipjack.com) and request that the Zoovy Developer serial number 100818546642 be associated with your SkipJack production account. If you are using Zoovy in conjunction with another shopping cart, then once you have changed your developer serial number for your production account you will be unable to perform credits using the other shopping cart.
  • Skipjack requires that a SHIPPING PHONE number be supplied with each order - so do not make shipping phone optional.
  • Skipjack settlements are known to take a long time to process, settlements can occur during a transaction (using Instant Capture), or afterwards (using Delayed Capture), for faster checkout times merchants are encouraged to use delayed capture mode with their Skipjack gateway.
  • Zoovy does not support the SkipJack DELETE/VOID feature via its API at this time, to perform a VOID please use the skipjack interface.
