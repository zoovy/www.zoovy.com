What to do if your gateway is not listed:
  1. Ask your merchant account provider to provide you with an industry standard gateway such as Paypal Payflow Pro, or Authorize.net. In our experience virtually every merchant account provider can support these industry standard gateways but don't "normally" refer users to them, and instead prefer to recommend more inexpensive, higher margin, less robust products.
  2. Zoovy has carefully vetted the list of gateways above based on transaction volume, security, and relative stability. We recommend you process with one of the gateways above. BPP Clients are required to use only the BPP recommended list.
  3. The cost of maintaining and providing support for an additional gateway integration is conservatively in the tens of thousands of dollars each year. For this reason we do not add gateways based on individual user requests unless the user or gateway is willing to pay for the upfront and ongoing integration costs - which would normally will be estimated at $10,000 upfront, and several thousand dollars annually.
  4. If your merchant account provider cannot use one of the gateways above we strongly encourage you to use Paypal Website Payments Pro, e-OnlineData + Authorize.Net, or Quickbooks Merchant Services.
