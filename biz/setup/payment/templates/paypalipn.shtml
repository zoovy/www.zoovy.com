Paypal will only insure funds for packages which are sent to a PayPal confirmed address. Unfortunately many buyers provide Zoovy with a non-PayPal Confirmed, alternative address for shipping. Zoovy can flag orders which are not being shipped to a verified address and put them in payment status "REVIEW" instead of payment status "PAID".

Setting up PayPal.com
Your Paypal.com account has a feature called "Instant Payment Notifications" (IPN) which sends a notification of each payment to a remote program such as Zoovy. Zoovy can use these payment notifications to automatically create orders for customers who did not use Zoovy Checkout to pay for their order.
  1. Login to your Paypal account
  2. Click on the "Profile" link
  3. Under Selling Preferences you will see a link for "Instant Payment Notification Preferences". Click that link.
  4. Click "edit link" and place the URL "http://paypal.zoovy.com/webapi/paypal/ipn.pl/prt=/" (without the quotes) into the space provided. Click the ON radio button.
  5. Now go to Profile / Shipping Calculations
  6. Check the box labelled "allow transaction-based shipping values to override the profile shipping settings listed above".
If the above steps are not completed, PayPal Payments will not function properly.
