Account Registration



If you DO NOT have an Overstock.com account, before continuing please go to http://www.overstock.com and create one.

Once you have an account at overstock.com, then login and provide a credit card, billing address and shipping address, as well as listing preferences such as which shipping methods are supported, and how tax is collected.

Once you have completed the steps above, then all you need to do is authorize Zoovy to access your Overstock Account. To do this click the button below.
