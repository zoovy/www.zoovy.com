Amazon Thesaurus

FACT: 95% of the purchases on Amazon are made through browse/search recommendation. Attempting to use Amazon without classifying your items is a waste of time and energy. Thesaurus Profiles (a Zoovy exclusive) make it easy to map your products to Amazon's Browse Nodes and keep them up to date.

Thesaurus / Item Classification Profiles:

Using thesaurus profiles dramatically reduces the time to both setup and maintain thesaraus keywords, thereby making it easier to have your products appear in more searches more often.

Item Type is required. However all other fields are optional, and in most cases will generate very little additional traffic to your items (except possibly target audience for items which could potentially be gifts).

There is some debate about the validity of Search Terms. Any Search Terms defined on the Product level will be combined with Search Terms on the Thesaurus level, with the Product level terms listed first.

If you ever have any questions about what information will help your sales, please contact your Amazon Sales Rep as this may change on a category by category basis.
