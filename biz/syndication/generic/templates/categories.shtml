Categories

Map Categories:
Rather than specifying categories for each product you can map the categories on your website to categories. These categories will be used in the event a specific category is not specified in the actual product.

Please note: Books, Movies, and Music are special categories which require a minimum of 50 distinct SKU's in order to be considered for submission. If you have less than 50 products you're products will not appear.
Batch Update:
Update all categories to the batch update category. The default category is root "."
However you may also specify safenames. ex: ".safename.subname"
