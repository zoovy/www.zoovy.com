Veruta Setup:

What is Veruta?
Veruta is a re-marketing service. The Veruta plugin will identify, and display your banners on other websites to customers who have visited your site, interacted with the site, and left without making a purchase. Veruta can be deployed for virtually any type of business and can be highly targeted to only re-target clients who have a high likelihood to purchase (such as only retargeting clients who have added something to their cart). Veruta will also track which items visitors showed interest in, and can use this information to display relevant/targeted special limited time offers which deliver a compelling call to action. In our opinion, there are many reasons why companies would want to use Veruta to try and pick up any missed sales opportunities (especially at the prices Zoovy clients receive).

How much does this cost?
Zoovy has a special relationship with Veruta. Our clients do not pay normal CPE (Cost Per Experience fees) which can be very expensive and difficult to measure. Instead Zoovy clients pay a flat $3.50 base CPM (Cost Per Impression) for branding/exposure, in addition to a 3% CPA (Cost Per Action/Sale) based on the Order Total. Zoovy clients also gain access to other exclusive veruta features which are normally reserved for large agencies with substantial budgets. Zoovy clients receive the same transaction rates as very large agencies, however unlike large agencies our clients do not pay a minimum fee. You will only pay for what you use, and a nominal rev. share to Veruta for the resulting sales your site generates. Veruta charges will appear on your normal Zoovy invoice.

Where will my banners appear?
Simply put: Veruta advertisements appear on sites your customers visit. Veruta will only show advertisements to people who have left without purchasing, and you only pay for advertising they deliver to your clients. Veruta currently reaches about 80% of the content sites on the Internet through its solid relationships with major media networks but the demographics vary based on your shoppers.

What is required from me?
Configuration is easy and takes just a few moments. There are no setup fees, beyond the cost of creating your remarketing messaging. To get started, Veruta requires 3 different creative banner sizes to be supplied for each profile (campaign). The preparing of the creatives require 4 steps: artwork, xml assembly, testing and upload.

To generate the necessary creatives you have the following options:

  1. Have Custom artwork created and assembled/tested/uploaded (Hourly Rates apply)
    Use this option if you would like to work 1:1 with a graphic designer to create an individually tailored campaign for your business. Custom artwork and integration will be able to access functionality such as dynamic re-pricing which is NOT offered in Express. For hourly rates please see webdoc http://webdoc.zoovy.com/doc/50551
  2. Supply your own artwork, and have it assembled/tested/uploaded by Zoovy (Hourly Rates apply)
    Use this option if you have specific graphic style constraints or guidelines supplied by your marketing or product brand management group which will need to be adhered to.
  3. [RECOMMENDED] Have Express artwork created and assembled/uploaded (One Time Fee: $75)
    With Express Artwork - a graphic designer will select an appropriate matching style which resembles your website and styling for your logo. This reduces the amount of customization, and subsequent testing which is necessary, and therefore the cost is able to be reduced as well. As with any express project you will not be given the opportunity to make changes prior to test/assembly, and any changes will be handled as custom hourly work after the project is completed.
