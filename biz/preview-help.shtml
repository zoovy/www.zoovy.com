Preview Help

The preview window displays a "what if" version of your website for a theme that you choose. Unfortunately due to the numerous different types of browsers on the market, there are issues which may occur when you attempt to preview.

If the page does not change in preview mode:
When a page does not update it is usually due to a common browser feature called "Caching" that Zoovy implements to improve website performance for your customers. Caching tells a web browser to keep a copy of a webpage on its local disk and not to request a fresh copy from the server for several hours. Unfortunately caching can get in the way while you are developing your website.

To force your browser to ignore the cached copy do the following steps:
On a PC using Internet Explorer - hold down the shift key and press "Reload"
On a PC using Netscape - hold down the shift key and press "Refresh"
On a Macintosh - hold down the shift key and RELOAD

After previewing page the page does not change back to the original:
This is most likely also caused by caching. In these circumstances follow the steps outlined above for reloading the web document.
