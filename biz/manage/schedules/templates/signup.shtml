Wholesale Signup Form
Usage: This area lets you configure a signup form, which is typically used to allow wholesale clients to register themselves for accounts on a store. The form fields are described in the JSON below, and can be customized to suite your specific needs. Fields such as resale/tax id, requested credit limit, credit references can be enabled/disabled by modifying changing "ignore":0 to "ignore":1 or vice versa depending on your needs. In addition the messaging displayed to the customer can be customized in setup / system messages, finally you can customize your notification preferences to determine how you are notified when a client uses the signup form.
