Standard Subscription Lists:
Subscriber lists come in three types: Opt-Out, Opt-In, and Private. Each data partition may have up to 15 subscriber lists. Many smaller companies will only have one subscriber list and never have a need for more than one. However larger companies with dedicated marketing staff will often have a monthly list, a weekly list, a daily list, and possibly even one or more "special deals" style lists. Customers can choose to subscribe to any list except a private list. Common examples of private lists include "Wholesale Clients", "Membership Rewards club", etc. and commonly access to these lists is gated by a membership or authentication system that is integrated with the store.
