Newsletters campaigns will activate and begin sending mail within 15 minutes of their scheduled time. The actual send rate will be determined by how many other campaigns are running concurrently, along with an embedded rate limiter per user to avoid being blocked by ISP spam filters. To anticipate how long a campaign should take to fully execute - use an output rate of approximately 250 emails every 10 minutes per Zoovy account.
