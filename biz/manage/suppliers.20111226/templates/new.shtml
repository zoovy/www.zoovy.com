Ordering Method
 
name="FORMAT" value="STOCK"> Stock: Inventory is shipped to your warehouse, rather than direct to customer. The system will attempt to maintain minimum inventory levels (if set) and will allow you to group multiple customer orders together into a single larger order to your supplier. Supplier SKU #'s (when available) are used to place orders.
 
name="FORMAT" value="FULFILL"> Fulfillment: New Orders should be transmitted to your fullfillment center, no attempt to hide or conceal data is performed. Your SKU #'s are used, rather than supplier SKU's.
 
name="FORMAT" value="DROPSHIP"> Drop Ship: Pricing information is not included, the customers shipping information is sent, but the billing information is replaced with your information. Contact email and phone numbers for the order are set to your store information. Supplier SKU #'s are substituted for your SKU #'s whenever available.
name="FORMAT" value="NONE"> None: No automatic ordering will be performed. You will need to manually place orders with this supplier.
Hint: If you have a single supplier where some items are drop ship and other items are stocked, you will need to create two different supplier codes in the Zoovy system (one for stock and one for drop ship).

JEDI allows you to download information from a report store. Transmission of orders, receiving automatic tracking updates. Optional shipping integration, and inventory downloads.

To use this functionality the supplier must have enabled your wholesale customer account (in their store) for JEDI integration.
name="MODE" value="API"> API Link
    API Link allows realtime seamless data exchange with a non-Zoovy system. API Link is commonly used when a programmer needs to modify/convert the native Zoovy order before it is sent to a supplier. Zoovy actively works with larger suppliers to establish a native Zoovy API Link interface for our community to use- contact your Supplier, or Zoovy Account Manager to learn more. *Requires the API bundle.
