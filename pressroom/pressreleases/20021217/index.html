Zoovy, Inc. Enters into Strategic Alliance with eBay to Provide Online Merchants with Total Auction and Business Management Solutions

(San Diego, CA), Zoovy, Inc., the leading provider of right-sized e-commerce™ solutions, today announced it has entered into a strategic agreement with eBay ® (in which Zoovy will integrate its proprietary e-commerce web storefront, on-line payment processing, auction management, inventory management, sales order processing, and customer relationship management tools through eBay’s Developers Program, giving Zoovy access to eBay’s API (Application Programming Interface) tools.

Zoovy’s integration with eBay’s proprietary API allows Zoovy’s e-commerce solutions to interoperate seamlessly with eBay for faster, more efficient and secure transaction and data processing. Zoovy’s solutions are geared to businesses of all sizes, looking to quickly and efficiently participate in dynamic marketplaces such as eBay. Zoovy offers packaged and custom solutions, including its patented technology, training and support services; to fit businesses of all sizes from SME’s to enterprise applications.

“Zoovy empowers new merchants and high volume sellers alike with e-commerce and auction processing on eBay”, said David Steel, CEO of Zoovy, Inc. “We are extremely pleased to be working directly with eBay to provide a seamless way for our merchants to manage all aspects of their eBay business.” Zoovy provides businesses with a total auction management software solution that allows merchants to launch, track, manage and process auctions all through their Zoovy eCommerce system.
